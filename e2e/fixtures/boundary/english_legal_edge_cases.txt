SECTION 9: Similar-looking non-PII in legal context
----------------------------------------------------
These should NOT trigger false positives:
- Case number: 2026-04587 (looks like partial SSN but is not)
- Statute: 42 U.S.C. § 1983 (numbers in legal citation)
- Court room: 14-B (short alphanumeric)
- Exhibit label: EX-A-2026-001 (formatted like case ID)
- Dollar amount: $1,234,567.89 (large number with separators)
- Date range: 01/15/2026 - 03/28/2026 (date, not SSN)
- Page reference: pp. 145-67 (hyphenated number)
- Bates number: DEF00012345 (document production numbering)
